Home Fit

HomeFit is a program developed by the Energy Efficiency and Conservation Authority (EECA) in New Zealand to help homeowners improve the energy efficiency, health, and comfort of their homes. It provides a comprehensive assessment of a home's energy efficiency, including insulation, heating, ventilation, and moisture control, and provides recommendations for improvements. The assessment is carried out by a trained and accredited HomeFit assessor, who provides a report detailing the current state of the home and recommended upgrades to improve its energy efficiency and overall comfort. HomeFit aims to help homeowners make informed decisions about home upgrades and to create healthier and more energy-efficient homes.

How to get home fitness assessment:

  • Home fitness assessments specialist will come to your home and assess your current fitness level, goals, and any limitations you may have. They will then create a customized fitness plan for you to follow at home.
  • Look for online resources that offer home fitness assessments. There are many fitness websites and apps that offer free or paid assessments to help you determine your fitness level and create a personalized workout plan.
  • Use a fitness tracker or wearable device that can help you track your fitness progress and provide recommendations for improving your fitness level. Many of these devices come with built-in fitness assessments and personalized workout plans.

We can assist you with making insulation improvements on your HomeFit journey, and provide a final assessment and report to certify that your home is conducive to the health and wellbeing of its occupants.
